About Me

I am Dr. G Someshwaran , a Doctorate(Ph.D) and Assistant Professor with a strong academic background and a deep interest in advanced computing technologies. I am have completed my Ph.D. in Image Processing using Deep Learning at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur, where my research focuses on developing intelligent and reliable solutions for real-world problems, particularly in the healthcare domain. I hold a Master’s degree in VLSI Design and a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. These qualifications have provided me with a strong foundation in both hardware and software systems, enabling me to approach research problems with a multidisciplinary perspective and a strong analytical mindset.

My core areas of specialization include Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and VLSI Design. I am actively involved in research, teaching, and student mentoring, with hands-on experience in applying advanced deep learning models and convolutional neural networks to medical image analysis and AI-driven healthcare applications. I have authored, co-authored and corresponding autohered multiple peer-reviewed journal and conference publications in reputed international venues. I am passionate about knowledge dissemination, academic excellence, and innovation-driven research, and I continuously strive to contribute to technology-enabled solutions that create meaningful impact in society while mentoring and inspiring the next generation of engineers and researchers.
