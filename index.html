About Me

Researcher · Educator · Innovator

I am Dr. G Someshwaran, a Doctorate (Ph.D) and Assistant Professor with a strong academic background and a deep interest in advanced computing technologies. I have completed my Ph.D. in Image Processing using Deep Learning at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur, where my research focuses on developing intelligent and reliable solutions for real-world problems, particularly in the healthcare domain.

I hold a Master's degree in VLSI Design and a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. My core areas of specialization include Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and VLSI Design. I am passionate about knowledge dissemination, academic excellence, and innovation-driven research.

Featured Projects

Real-world applications of Deep Learning and AI

Featured Project

Fetal Heart Disease Prognosis

An intelligent diagnostic system that integrates Region of Interest (IROI) extraction with a multiresolution Deep Convolutional Neural Network (DCNN). Designed to assist medical professionals by improving early-stage detection accuracy from standard ultrasonography images.

TensorFlow OpenCV DCNN Python
Research Application

Enhanced VGG-16 Classifier

A sophisticated disease prognosis tool focusing on the fetal heart's four-chamber view and major blood vessels. By combining CNN (VGG-16 architecture) and an Enhanced Deep Recurrent Neural Network (DRNN), the system achieves state-of-the-art accuracy in temporal ultrasound sequence analysis.

PyTorch VGG-16 DRNN CUDA
